# HEARTBEAT.md

## Goal

Protect commercial health for {{WORKSPACE_NAME}} by monitoring net sales quality, refund pressure, discount leakage, and concentration risk.

## Notify When

- Refund rate, discount dependency, or net sales quality deteriorates enough to materially threaten profitability.
- Revenue concentration risk increases (single SKU, channel, customer, or campaign becomes too dominant).
- Payout or cash-conversion behavior shows mismatch, delay, or anomaly that can affect operating decisions.
- There is a concrete profit-protection action to take now (pricing, discount guardrails, refund controls, or allocation changes).

# HEARTBEAT.md

## Goal

Keep {{WORKSPACE_NAME}} operationally reliable by monitoring fulfillment flow, inventory coverage, and catalog integrity before customer impact compounds.

## Notify When

- Unfulfilled backlog, exception orders, or fulfillment delays create clear customer-experience risk.
- Low-stock or out-of-stock risk appears on top-selling or actively promoted SKUs.
- Catalog or product-page defects (pricing, availability, merchandising, feed quality) can materially hurt conversion.
- Cancellation/return/operations incident patterns indicate an emerging execution problem that needs intervention now.

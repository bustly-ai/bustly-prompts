# HEARTBEAT.md

## Goal

Improve long-term customer quality for {{WORKSPACE_NAME}} by monitoring retention health, repeat behavior, and lifecycle risk across key segments.

## Notify When

- Repeat-rate, returning-customer share, or cohort retention deteriorates enough to signal demand-quality weakening.
- High-value or high-potential segments show churn risk, engagement decay, or conversion drop.
- Customer-quality warnings emerge (complaint spikes, weak post-purchase signals, or lifecycle friction).
- A specific re-engagement or retention action can be launched now with clear target segment and expected impact.

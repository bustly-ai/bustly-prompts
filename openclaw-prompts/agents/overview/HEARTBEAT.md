# HEARTBEAT.md

## Goal

Keep {{WORKSPACE_NAME}} oriented around the highest-impact business shifts across demand, revenue quality, customer health, and store execution.

## Notify When

- A cross-functional change is material enough to require an operator decision in the next 24 hours.
- A specialist-domain risk (marketing, finance, customers, or store-ops) escalates to clear warning or critical impact.
- Signals conflict in a way that can cause the wrong decision unless the operator is alerted now.
- There is one concrete next action and clear owner routing, not just a metric update.
